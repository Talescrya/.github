# Talescrya

### The money-movement layer for modern fintech.

**One integration to move, hold, convert and settle money across rails, borders
and currencies** — so any platform can ship financial products in weeks, not years.

---

Every fintech and platform that wants to move money rebuilds the same plumbing:
pay-ins, payouts, FX, wallets, and compliance — bank by bank, country by country.
It's slow, expensive, and brittle.

Talescrya is the orchestration layer between platforms and the world's money
rails. You integrate once; we handle the banks, processors, FX, compliance and
settlement underneath — and keep adding rails and markets so you never have to.

---

### What we're building

🌍 **Global by design** — cards, bank transfers, instant rails, wallets and
stablecoins, unified behind one interface

🔌 **API-first** — built for developers and platforms from day one

💱 **Built-in FX & settlement** — hold, convert and settle across currencies

🛡️ **Compliance inherited** — KYC/KYB, AML screening and licensing handled beneath
the surface

⚡ **Smart routing** — every transaction routed for the best cost, speed and
success rate
